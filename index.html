Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about VoiceText Pro

How accurate is the speech recognition?

VoiceText Pro achieves 99%+ accuracy using advanced AI algorithms. Accuracy may vary based on audio quality, accent, and speaking clarity. The system continuously learns and improves with use.

Which languages are supported?

Currently, VoiceText Pro supports Hindi and English with excellent accent recognition. We're working on adding more languages based on user demand.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely! All speech processing happens locally in your browser. No audio data is sent to our servers. Your conversations and transcripts remain completely private and secure.

Can I export my transcripts?

Yes! You can export your transcripts in multiple formats including TXT, and PDF. You can also copy to clipboard or share directly with others.

Do I need to install any software?

No installation required! VoiceText Pro works directly in your web browser. Simply grant microphone permission and start transcribing immediately.

What are voice commands?

Voice commands let you format your text hands-free. Say "comma" for punctuation, "new line" for breaks, "period" for full stops, and many more formatting commands while speaking.

Can I use it on mobile devices?

Yes! VoiceText Pro is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface adapts to your screen size for optimal usability.

Does any information about VoiceTextPro support voice-to-text conversion on mobile browsers?

VoiceTextPro does not support voice-to-text conversion on mobile browsers; this feature works only on desktop browsers, while all other features work on mobile.

Is there a limit to transcription time?

There's no time limit! You can transcribe for as long as needed. The app includes pause/resume functionality so you can take breaks during long sessions.

How to Use VoiceText Pro

Follow these simple steps to get the most out of our advanced speech recognition technology

1

Grant Microphone Permission

When you first visit the transcribe page, your browser will ask for microphone permission. Click "Allow" to enable speech recognition. This is required for the app to hear your voice and convert it to text.

Browser will show permission dialog

2

Choose Your Language

Select your preferred language from the dropdown menu. We support Hindi and English with excellent accent recognition and regional dialect support.

Select Hindi or English

3

Configure Settings

Enable optional features like noise reduction, auto-save, dark mode, and voice punctuation commands for the best experience.

Customize settings for optimal experience

4

Start Recording

Click the "Start" button to begin transcription. You'll see the recording indicator and audio visualizer. Speak clearly at a normal pace for best results.

Click start and begin speaking

5

Use Voice Commands

While speaking, use voice commands for punctuation and formatting: "comma", "period", "question mark", "new line", "new paragraph", and more.

Complete voice command library available

6

Export Your Transcript

Export your transcript in TXT or PDF format. You can also copy to clipboard or share directly with others.

Export in multiple professional formats
